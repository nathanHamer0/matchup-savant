Matchup Savant

Welcome! This is a baseball batter-pitcher matchup calculator. The way it works is: based on respective per-pitch-type and per-zone run-value (RV/100) splits for both batter and pitcher, a comprehensive matchup score is calibrated, considering both arsenal and location factors. Simply select a batter-pitcher duo and press the 'Matchup!' button to generate the matchup score and associated graphics. That's it!
